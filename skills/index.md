# Skills

Modular capabilities for the research orchestrator.

- `arxiv/`: Procedures for arXiv submission.
- `audio/`: Text-to-speech and audio processing.
- `issues-sync/`: Bidirectional GitHub Issue synchronization.
- `receipts/`: Formal validation of research velocity.
- `tex-pdf/`: LaTeX to PDF compilation pipeline.
- `triage/`: [Epistemic Triage (SKILL.md)](triage/SKILL.md) framework.
- `video/`: Video asset management.
- `zenodo/`: Archiving and DOI assignment.
