A long-form spoken-word deep dive recorded while the researcher navigated Amsterdam by bike, hands-free. Claude walks the Academic Vibing corpus (v0.5/0.6) and the "5 Papers in 4 Days" receipts paper, covering:

- **Consensus Poisoning** — agreement is now free to manufacture; consensus among LLMs reflects shared bias, not validation.
- **The Leapfrog Mechanism** — the GitHub Issue-Loop as an exocognitive state store (Clark & Chalmers reframe); re-entry drops from ~23 min to ~3 min; the operator is not faster, but freer.
- **@guninvalid (Issue #51)** — authentic confusion as the only un-forgeable CDI signal; co-authorship belongs to whoever breaks the consensus trap.
- **Rock Culture archetypes** — Pirates, Malones, Pakleds; proficiency cloaking vs. genuine confusion.
