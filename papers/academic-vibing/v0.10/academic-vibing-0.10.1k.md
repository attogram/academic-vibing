# Consensus Divergence Index (CDI): Measuring Signal in Multi-Model LLM Swarms (1k context)

CDI = d/n (disputed/total). Metric for LLM research signal.
High CDI (>0.5) = Research Boundary (Signal).
Low CDI (<0.1) = Consensus Trap (Bias).
Leapfrog Effect: Persistent Issue-Loop reduces context re-entry (23m -> <3m).
RAC: Recursive Agent Consensus auditing.
Zero-budget viability on consumer hardware (16GB RAM).
Synthesis of Attogram Corpus (P0).
