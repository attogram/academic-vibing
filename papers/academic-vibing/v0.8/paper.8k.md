# Academic Vibing 0.8: Structured Curiosity for Human-Agent Collaboration (8k context)

Academic Vibing 0.8 represents the maturation of a rapid-iteration research methodology designed for Large Language Models (LLMs) and autonomous agents. This version synthesizes the foundational fragments developed across versions 0.1 through 0.7—including early Rock Talk protocols, Wikipedia and Hacker News "Institutional Trauma" post-mortems, and the formalization of the Consensus Divergence Index (CDI) as the primary metric for research signaling.

**Methodology: The RAC with CDI**
Centered on the Recursive Agent-Based Consensus Network (RAC), multiple disparate LLM agents (Jules, Gemini, Claude) explore a problem space. Consensus among these models serves as a proxy for peer review, while disagreement indicates areas requiring human intervention. CDI = (Disagreement / Total Claims). High CDI indicates high-signal "friction points"; Low CDI flags potential "Consensus Traps" or shared bias.

**The AI-Native Issue-Loop**
Utilizes GitHub issues as persistent externalized long-term memory. Standardized schema ([STATE], [BLOCKER], [PAYLOAD]) allow for headless MAS coordination and context maintenance regardless of session decay. Solving the "Token-to-Meaning Fallacy" by distinguishing between Instructional Transfer (External Rock Talk) and Cognitive Processing (Internal CoT).

**The Leapfrog Mechanism (F2)**
Refined underlying mechanism for productivity-wellbeing surplus. Not about speed, but frictionless context transfer. Ability to drop task (persists in Loop) and re-enter at zero cost (~3 min vs UCI's 23 min baseline). Wellbeing is causally downstream of re-entry freedom. Operator is not faster; operator is freer.

**Adversarial Rigor (E8/E10)**
Institutional Trauma at Wikipedia (Proof of Citation) and Hacker News (Proof of Vibe). MAS simulation of "Synthetic Interestingness" and "Sludge Floods" overwhelms human curators. Defense: Friction-First auditing (CDI). Simple agreement across models is a warning sign of shared training echoes.

**Mascot: ROCK the Puppy 🐶**
Symbolizing the "untrained but enthusiastic" nature of early-stage agentic swarms requiring human guidance.
