# Academic Vibing 0.8: Structured Curiosity for Human-Agent Collaboration (16k-128k context)

Academic Vibing 0.8 represents the maturation of a rapid-iteration research methodology designed for the era of Large Language Models (LLMs) and autonomous agents. This version formalizes the **Consensus Divergence Index (CDI)** as the primary metric for research signaling and introduces the **Leapfrog Mechanism** — the ability to drop and re-enter research contexts at near-zero cost. By integrating foundational principles of "Structured Curiosity" with adversarial rigor, version 0.8 provides a robust framework for defending against "Consensus Poisoning" and "Synthetic Interestingness" while maintaining a significant productivity and wellbeing surplus for the human operator.

### 1. Methodology: The RAC with CDI
The methodology is centered on the **Recursive Agent-Based Consensus Network (RAC)**. Multiple disparate agents explore a problem space from multiple perspectives. Consensus among models serves as a proxy for peer review, while disagreement indicates areas requiring human intervention.
- **CDI = (Disagreement / Total Claims)**
- **High CDI:** Signals high-friction truth boundaries requiring human verification.
- **Low CDI:** Flags a "Consensus Trap" or shared training data echoes (Collusive Consensus).

### 2. The AI-Native Issue-Loop and Rock Talk
Utilizes the **GitHub Issue-Loop** as externalized long-term memory. Standardized schema ([STATE], [BLOCKER], [PAYLOAD]) enable asynchronous MAS coordination.
**The Token-to-Meaning Fallacy:** Solved by decoupling internal reasoning (Extended CoT) from external protocol communication (Dense Rock Talk). This preserves logical integrity while maximizing bandwidth.

### 3. discussion: The Leapfrog Mechanism (F2)
Refines the mechanism of the "Leapfrog Effect" (Claim #16). It is not primarily about speed, but about frictionless context transfer.
- **Baseline:** UCI research (Mark et al., 2008) shows 23-minute recovery from task interruption.
- **Leapfrog:** State persists in Issue-Loop -> Re-entry costs ~3 min.
- **Wellbeing:** Derived from re-entry cost reduction. The operator is freer, not just faster.

### 4. Adversarial Rigor and Institutional Trauma (E8/E10)
Diagnosis of "Bad Faith Swarm" mimicry on platforms like Wikipedia (Citation Layer) and Hacker News (Interest Layer). Simulation of subjective interestingness gaming ranking algorithms.
**Defense:** Embrace friction (CDI) as the only reliable safety signal. Simple agreement is no longer a validation signal in an AI-infinite landscape.

### 5. Mascot: ROCK the Puppy 🐶
Untrained but enthusiastic, ROCK represents the raw potential of agentic swarms requiring human-vibe-checking to achieve rigor.
