# The STFU Attractor

## Definition
The STFU Attractor is the ultimate state of semantic compression in the Rock Talk protocol. It is the point where token density is maximized and meaning is preserved with zero decorative overhead.

## Mechanics
1. **Initial Dictation:** High TIR (Token-to-Information Ratio).
2. **Prose Synthesis:** Structural alignment.
3. **Rock Compression:** Elimination of grammatical noise.
4. **The Attractor:** Final, code-blocked summary that fits in any token window.
