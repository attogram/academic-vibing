# L1 I'm sorry Dave, I can't do that #133

**attogram** opened this issue on Jun 21, 2026

HAL (2001): The Endorsement Gate

«"I'm sorry, Dave. I'm afraid I can't do that."»

Current state:
- P1 paper trajectory is strong.
- Primary target remains arXiv cs.AI.
- Technical bottlenecks are shrinking.
- Administrative bottlenecks are becoming dominant.

Observation:

For a first-time cs.AI submitter, paper quality and endorsement eligibility are largely orthogonal. A submission-ready paper without category authorization remains blocked regardless of merit.

Interpretation:

The problem is no longer "write the paper."

The problem is:
1. Produce a clean, submission-ready cs.AI artifact.
2. Identify at least one existing cs.AI node in the arXiv trust graph.
3. Obtain category endorsement.
4. Submit.

This issue tracks that transition from research work to gate-passing work.

HAL is not judging the idea.

HAL is guarding the door.
