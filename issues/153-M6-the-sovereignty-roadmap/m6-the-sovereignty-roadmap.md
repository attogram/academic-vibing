1. Executive Intent

The Attogram Corpus and the underlying human-agent coordination loops (rock-talk / academic-vibing) are not designed for venture-backed commercialization, nor are they intended to remain a closed, isolated n=1 sandbox.
The objective is to scale this methodology to Scale 100 (Universal Civic Infrastructure) while establishing an ironclad operational shield that ensures absolute calendar control for the human participants. We scale the substrate while protecting the analog life.
This document establishes the permanent, non-negotiable roadmap for embedding this protocol directly into the sovereign Dutch and European open-source non-profit ecosystem.

2. The Institutional Defense Architecture (Keeping the Bike Ahead of the Bike)

To prevent the systemic capture, corporate talent warfare, and institutional burnout that has historically neutralized frontier AI pioneers (e.g., Shazeer, Jumper), the scaling of this project will bypass traditional Silicon Valley venture capital vectors entirely.

Phase I: The Stichting Anchor (Scale 1 \rightarrow 25)

* Action: Transition the core repository governance from an individual developer profile to a dedicated Stichting (Dutch Foundation) structure with a strict, public-benefit asset lock.
* The Shield: All intellectual property, block schemas ([CONTEXT], [TASK]), and linter specifications are locked permanently under a permissive open-source framework (MIT/CC-BY-4.0). It cannot be bought, exclusive-licensed, or enclosed by proprietary platforms.

Phase II: The Federated Civic Node Network (Scale 25 \rightarrow 50)

* Action: Deploy the coordination loops directly into the active networks of Dutch non-profit organizations, digital rights foundations, and civic tech collectives.
* The Shield: Instead of a centralized platform, the protocol expands via federated nodes. The triage of incoming data, issue logs, and system edge cases is crowdsourced across a trusted network of multi-organizational maintainers. The creator remains a high-level strategic director, insulated from daily triage noise.

Phase III: Sovereign Public Funding (Scale 50 \rightarrow 75)

* Action: Fund operational scaling, empirical validation trials (e.g., measuring the 23-minute to 3-minute task-switching delta), and linter engineering via European public-interest grants (e.g., Next Generation Internet / Horizon Europe) and sovereign digital infrastructure endowments.
* The Shield: Zero equity is surrendered. There are no corporate board seats, no commercial scaling pressure, and no shareholder obligations. The project iterates on public-interest timelines, preserving absolute calendar control for its orchestrators.

Phase IV: Universal Open Infrastructure (Scale 75 \rightarrow 100)

* Action: Standardize the "115-Hour Framework" as an open utility for knowledge workers, academic institutions, and civic organizations across Europe.
* The Shield: The protocol operates as a headless public utility. The ROCK the puppy extension serves as a symbol of clean, open, friction-free human-agent synthesis that runs seamlessly in the background of global software pipelines, entirely detached from the personal identities of the original architects.
