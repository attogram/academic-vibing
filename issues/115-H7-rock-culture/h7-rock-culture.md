TV episodes (Samaritan Snare, Unfrozen Caveman Lawyer) are formatted
identically to peer-reviewed/arXiv citations in the References list.
Not wrong to cite a TV episode as a cultural touchstone — but visually
conflating it with academic sources is the same credibility-blur problem

"Cultural References" and "Research Citations" — so a reader can tell
at a glance which is which.

not validation). These should cross-reference each other once both
papers are stable, rather than each claiming the concept in isolation.
